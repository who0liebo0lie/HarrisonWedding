FAQ

Answers before you sail

Can I bring another person on the cruise?

We're so looking forward to spending time on the boat with our family and friends! If there are family or friends you'd like to extend the invitation to, please do. Just be aware that, due to space limitations, only invited wedding guests can be included in the wedding events themselves.

What travel documents do I need?

For this closed-loop sailing, U.S. citizens can travel with a birth certificate plus a Real ID. A valid passport also works and is recommended if you have one.

Are kids welcome?

Yes! Children are welcome on the cruise and it's very much a family celebration. (The wedding events are reserved for invited wedding guests — see above.)

How do I book my room?

All cabins are booked through our travel partner, Aligned Journeys, at a negotiated group rate. Head to the Travel & Staterooms page to see the options. Final payment is due at the end of October 2026.

Is there a gift registry?

Your presence is the present — truly. Nothing is expected. For anyone who asks, we'll have Zelle details available, but please don't feel any obligation.

What should I pack / wear?

Three looks: all-white for the Welcome Party, Sunset Sparkle (bright pinks & oranges) for the wedding, and playful jungle vibes for the after-party. Details on the Attire page.

What's included in the cruise fare?

Your stateroom, main dining and buffet meals, entertainment, and most onboard activities are included. Specialty dining, drinks packages, spa, and shore excursions are extra.

Who do I contact with questions?

For booking and travel logistics, contact Aligned Journeys. For anything wedding-related, reach out to Wesley & Julia directly.
