Tap each guest → your Messages app opens, pre-written. Just hit send.

1. Your site link

Paste your real GitHub Pages or harrisonsetsail2027.com address.

2. The message

{name} and {url} fill in automatically for each person.

3. Your guest list

Copy/paste straight from a spreadsheet — commas or tabs both work.

4. Send them

QR code for your link

Handy for save-the-dates, a printed card, or showing someone in person.

A few tips:
  • Send individually, not as one group text — group threads get chaotic fast and everyone gets every reply.
  • Do it from your own phone. Bulk-texting services (EZ Texting, Twilio, etc.) require registration and proof people opted in, which personal invites don't have.
  • Send in batches of 20–30 so carriers don't flag you for spam.
  • Always include the "enter your full name" line — otherwise guests hit the gate and get stuck.
