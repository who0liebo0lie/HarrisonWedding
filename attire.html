📌 How to add your Pinterest photos:
  1. Make a folder called attire next to this file.
  2. Save your Pinterest images into it as look-1.jpg, look-2.jpg, and so on.
  3. In attire.html, find each <div class="shot"> and swap the comment line inside it for the image tag it shows.
  4. Upload the attire folder along with your other files.

Add or delete shot blocks to change how many photos appear — the grid adjusts on its own.
